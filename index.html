Getting Started

How It Works

1

Register

Sign up on MyRaceTime and choose your tier: Digital, Medal Pack, or Complete Pack.

2

Connect

Link Strava, Apple Health, Google Fit, Fitbit, or Samsung Health to track your activities.

3

Move

Run, walk, or cycle throughout the quarter. Every kilometre counts toward your target.

4

Upload

Upload your activities through MyRaceTime. They appear as Pending until verified by our team.

5

Collect

Medals and shirts are shipped or available at collection points after each quarter closes.

Step-by-Step

How to Upload Activities

Guides for uploading your runs, walks, and rides across every platform.

MyRaceTime Desktop

Using a laptop or desktop computer

  1. 1
    Log in to your MyRaceTime account.
  2. 2
    Navigate to and open the event you want to upload your virtual activity for.
  3. 3
    Click “CONNECT STRAVA & UPLOAD” or “CONNECT STRAVA”.
  4. 4
    Connect your Strava account to MyRaceTime.
  5. 5
    Make sure “View data about your activities” permission is enabled.
  6. 6
    Click “Authorize” to continue.
  7. 7
    Click “UPLOAD ACTIVITIES”.
  8. 8
    Select all the activities you want to upload.
  9. 9
    Click “UPLOAD SELECTED”.
  10. 10
    Uploaded activities remain in Pending status until verified by an admin.
  11. 11
    Once approved, the status changes to Verified ✓
1 / 8

MyRaceTime Mobile Browser

Using a smartphone or tablet browser

  1. 1
    Tap the 3-line menu icon at the top right corner of the homepage.
  2. 2
    Log in to your MyRaceTime account.
  3. 3
    Navigate to Events and click “VIEW DETAILS”.
  4. 4
    Scroll down and click “CONNECT STRAVA & UPLOAD” or “CONNECT STRAVA”.
  5. 5
    Connect your Strava account to MyRaceTime.
  6. 6
    Enable “View data about your activities”.
  7. 7
    Click “Authorize”.
  8. 8
    Click “UPLOAD ACTIVITIES”.
  9. 9
    Select all the activities you want to upload.
  10. 10
    Click “UPLOAD SELECTED”.
  11. 11
    Activities remain Pending until verified by an admin.
  12. 12
    Once approved, the status changes to Verified ✓
1 / 9

MyRaceTime Mobile App

Android & iOS

  1. 1
    Log in to the MyRaceTime app and navigate to Profile.
  2. 2
    Scroll down to “Joined Races”.
  3. 3
    Select the event you want to upload activities for.
  4. 4
    Click “Add +”.
  5. 5
    Click “Submit”.
  6. 6
    Choose Strava, Apple Health, Fitbit, Google Fit, or Samsung Health.
  7. 7
    Click “GO TO CONNECT APPS”.
  8. 8
    Connect and authorize your preferred app.
  9. 9
    For Strava, ensure “View data about your activities” is enabled.
  10. 10
    Grant all requested permissions for other integrations.
  11. 11
    Return to “Joined Races”.
  12. 12
    Click “Add +” then “Submit”.
  13. 13
    Select all activities you want to upload.
  14. 14
    Click “UPLOAD”.
  15. 15
    Activities remain Pending until verified by an admin.
  16. 16
    Once approved, the status changes to Verified ✓
⚠ Important: Only connect and upload activities from ONE connected app. Uploading duplicate activities from multiple apps may result in your account being flagged or suspended.
